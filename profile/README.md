# BrazilCo AI Platform

> The AI engineering division of [BrazilCo](https://github.com/BrazilCo) — building production-grade AI infrastructure for Brazilian and global enterprises.

---

## 🏢 Who We Are

**BrazilCo** is a Brazilian software engineering and technology company building end-to-end digital solutions — from enterprise systems and cloud-native platforms to cutting-edge AI products.

**BrazilCo AI Platform** is the dedicated AI engineering division within BrazilCo. We design, build, and operate the foundational AI infrastructure that powers intelligent products across the company and its clients.

We bring together **software engineering excellence** and **applied AI research** to deliver systems that are reliable, scalable, and production-ready from day one.

---

## 🧱 Our Engineering Domains

### Artificial Intelligence & ML
Designing intelligent systems grounded in solid engineering principles.

| Component | Description |
|---|---|
| **AI Gateway** | Unified API layer for LLM routing, rate limiting, and observability |
| **Agent Framework** | Orchestration engine for multi-step autonomous AI agents |
| **ML Pipelines** | Scalable training, fine-tuning, and inference workflows |
| **RAG Infrastructure** | Vector databases, embedding pipelines, and retrieval systems |
| **AI Observability** | Tracing, evaluation, and monitoring for AI systems in production |

### Software Engineering & Platform
Building the foundation that makes AI systems reliable at scale.

| Component | Description |
|---|---|
| **Cloud & DevOps** | Infrastructure as code, CI/CD pipelines, and cloud-native architecture |
| **Data Platform** | Feature stores, data lakes, and real-time streaming pipelines |
| **API & Backend** | High-performance services powering AI-driven products |
| **Security & Compliance** | Enterprise-grade access control, audit trails, and data sovereignty |
